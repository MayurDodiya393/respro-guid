## 📌 What is Respro?

**Respro** is a modern business management system that helps you organize, track, and complete work tasks automatically. Think of it as a **smart checklist and task manager** that replaces spreadsheets and manual tracking.

Instead of using Google Sheets and emails to track who is doing what, Respro:
- ✅ **Automatically creates tasks** on schedule
- ✅ **Reminds people** when work is due
- ✅ **Tracks completion** and delays
- ✅ **Scores performance** in real-time
- ✅ **Works on mobile and desktop**

---

## 🎯 Main Features (What You Can Do)

### 1. **Daily Checklists** — Same tasks every day
Example: "Morning inspection", "Check inventory", "Close register"

### 2. **Weekly Checklists** — Tasks on certain days
Example: "Monday staff meeting", "Friday audit report"

### 3. **Monthly Tasks** — Once per month
Example: "Monthly reconciliation", "Safety audit"

### 4. **Workflow Steps** — Multi-step processes
Example: "Purchase Order → Approval → Delivery → Invoice"

### 5. **Automatic Scoring** — See who is performing well
Example: Green = Excellent, Yellow = Good, Red = Needs help

### 6. **Notifications** — Get reminders automatically
Email and in-app alerts before tasks are due

## 📋 How to Use: Step-by-Step Examples

### **Scenario 1: Daily Site Checklist**

**What:** Every morning, you need to check the construction site.

**Old way (Google Sheets):**
- Open Sheet
- Find today's date
- Mark complete manually
- Hope someone doesn't forget
- Send email reminders

**New way (Respro):**

1. **Admin sets it up once** (not your job)
   - Checklist name: "Morning Site Inspection"
   - When: Every weekday at 8:00 AM
   - Who: All site engineers
   - Reminder: 30 min before

2. **You receive tasks automatically**
   - 7:30 AM → Email: "Your task starts in 30 minutes"
   - App shows **1 pending task** with red banner
   - You tap "Complete" and add notes: "All clear, no issues"
   - ✅ Done. Score updated.

3. **Your manager sees results** (real-time)
   - Dashboard shows: "Ravi completed 22/22 inspections (100%)" 🟢 **GREEN**

---

### **Scenario 2: Weekly Team Meeting Report**

**What:** Every Friday, you submit a weekly report.

**Setup (Admin):**
- Checklist: "Friday Weekly Report"
- When: Every Friday by 5:00 PM
- Who: All department heads
- What to fill: "Number of orders", "Issues faced", "Next week plan"

**You do it:**
1. Friday 4:00 PM → App notification: "Your report is due in 1 hour"
2. Click **Complete** button → Small form appears
3. Fill 3 fields (takes 2 minutes)
4. Hit **Submit** ✅
5. Form auto-sends to your boss
6. Score: On-time ✅ = +100 points this week

---

### **Scenario 3: Purchase Order Approval Process** (Multi-step)

**What:** Every PO goes through 4 steps: Submit → Manager Approval → Accounts Check → Delivery.

**Step-by-step:**

| Step | What | Who | When |
|------|------|-----|------|
| 1 | Create PO | You (Buyer) | Same day by 3 PM |
| 2 | Manager Reviews | Manager | Next day by 10 AM |
| 3 | Accounts Verifies | Accounts | Same day by 5 PM |
| 4 | Delivery Approval | Stores | 2 days before delivery |

**How it works:**
- You create PO (form auto-fills: vendor, amount, delivery date)
- ✅ Submitted — System auto-notifies Manager
- Manager approves or rejects
- ✅ Approved — System auto-notifies Accounts
- Accounts checks budget and approves
- ✅ Approved — System auto-notifies Stores
- Stores confirms → PO complete
- Delay tracking automatic (if any step is late, system tracks it)

### What the Colors Mean

| 🟢 GREEN | 🟡 YELLOW | 🔴 RED |
|---------|----------|--------|
| **90+** | **70–89** | **Below 70** |
| Excellent! | Good | Needs attention |

### How Scoring Works (Simple Version)

```
Score = (Tasks Completed On Time × 100)
        − (Late tasks × 20)
        − (Missed tasks × 50)
```

**Example This Week:**
- 22 on-time tasks = **22 points**
- 2 late tasks = **−40 points**
- 1 missed = **−50 points**
- **Total = 88 points** = 🟡 YELLOW

## 🎓 Practical Walkthrough: Create Your First Checklist

**Scenario:** You want to track "Daily Security Patrol" for your guards.

### Step 1: Create the Master (Admin)
1. Go to **Masters → Checklists**
2. Click **+ New Checklist**
3. Fill:
   - **Name:** "Morning Security Patrol"
   - **Code:** "SECURITY_PATROL"
   - **Department:** Security
   - **Priority:** High
   - **Expected time:** 30 minutes
4. Click **Save** ✅

### Step 2: Set Schedule (Admin)
1. Go to **Checklist → Configure Recurrence**
2. Pick your checklist
3. Set:
   - **Frequency:** Daily
   - **Working days:** Mon–Fri only (uncheck Sat, Sun)
   - **Time due:** 8:00 AM
   - **Skip weekends:** Yes
   - **Holiday skip:** Yes
   - **Reminder before:** 15 minutes
4. Click **Save** ✅

### Step 3: Assign People (Admin)
1. Go to **Assignments**
2. Pick your checklist
3. Click **+ Add**
4. Choose:
   - **Assignment type:** ROLE
   - **Role:** Security Guard
   - (Or select specific people)
5. Click **Save** ✅

### Step 4: Check Results (Next day)
- All Security Guards get notified at 7:45 AM
- They open app → See "Morning Security Patrol"
- They mark **Complete** with notes if needed
- ✅ Score updates automatically

**That's it!** No more manual tracking.

## 🆚 Why Respro Instead of Google Sheets?

### The Old Way (Google Sheets)

| Problem | Impact |
|---------|--------|
| ❌ Manual entry of dates | Takes 5+ min per task, prone to errors |
| ❌ Email reminders (someone must send) | People forget, or get 100 emails |
| ❌ No automatic notifications | Staff don't know about tasks |
| ❌ Scoring done manually | Admin updates every week (hours of work) |
| ❌ No accountability | Hard to see who actually did what |
| ❌ Mobile-unfriendly | Can't access on phone easily |
| ❌ Delays not tracked | No way to know if people are slow |
| ❌ Change is painful | If you move a due date, redo all rows |
| ❌ Holiday handling manual | Forget a holiday = wrong due dates |
| ❌ No history | Old data deleted or hard to find |

**Time wasted:** 2–4 hours per week per admin

### The Respro Way

| Solution | Benefit |
|----------|---------|
| ✅ Auto-generated tasks | 1-time setup, runs forever |
| ✅ Smart reminders | Right person, right time, right channel |
| ✅ Instant notifications | Alerts via email, app, even WhatsApp |
| ✅ Real-time scoring | Dashboard updates automatically |
| ✅ Built-in accountability | Every action logged with timestamp |
| ✅ Mobile app | Works on any phone, online or offline |
| ✅ Automatic delay tracking | System calculates delays, no manual work |
| ✅ Easy edits | Change one rule, all future tasks update |
| ✅ Holiday-aware | Just add dates to holiday master, system handles it |
| ✅ Full audit trail | Every task, comment, and revision saved |

**Time saved:** 8–10 hours per week per admin

## 🛠️ Technology Stack & Why It Matters

### What Runs Respro

**Database:** PostgreSQL (enterprise database)
- Stores millions of tasks without slowing down
- Automatic backups and recovery
- Works for 100+ users at once

**Backend:** NestJS (modern server)
- Reliable and fast
- Scales as you grow
- Real-time notifications possible

**Frontend:** React (modern web app)
- Works on all devices
- Fast and responsive
- Professional UI like you see on modern apps (Slack, Figma, etc.)

**Hosting:** Cloud-based
- Available 24/7
- Automatic updates
- No server maintenance on your end

## 🐛 Troubleshooting (Common Issues)

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| "I didn't get a notification" | Check app notification settings; ask admin to check reminder config |
| "Task due date looks wrong" | Check holiday master (might be a holiday); check time zone settings |
| "My score dropped suddenly" | Missed task counts as −50 points; check task history |
| "Checklist didn't create" | Refresh page; ask admin to regenerate tasks |
| "Can't see other department's tasks" | Access is limited by role; ask admin to grant access |
| "Form won't submit" | Check if all **required** fields are filled (marked with \*) |

**For more help:** Contact your admin or IT support team.
